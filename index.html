Records your screen — switch apps, everything is captured.

💡 How to present: when the share window opens, choose Window or Chrome Tab and pick your teaching content — never "Entire Screen" (blocked, it causes a mirror effect) and never MaherCast itself. Then tap 📌 in the toolbar: your tools pop into a small floating panel that stays on top of everything while you present. Draw on its preview — annotations land in the video, the panel itself never does.

🪄 While recording: magic pencil, arrows, notes, focus spotlight, live captions, bubble styles & face filters — all baked into your video.

⚠️ Screen capture isn't available in this browser (iPhones don't allow it). Camera mode has you covered.
